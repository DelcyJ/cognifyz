WhatsApp

WhatsApp is a messaging app for smartphones and other devices. It allows users to send text messages and voice messages, make voice and video calls, and share images, documents, user locations, and other content.

Instagram

Instagram is a popular social media platform known for its emphasis on visual storytelling. Users can share photos and videos, follow friends and celebrities, and explore trending content through hashtags and curated feeds.

YouTube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform where users can watch, like, share, comment, and upload their own videos. The platform provides a wide range of content, including music videos, tutorials, vlogs, and more.

Snapchat

Snapchat is a multimedia messaging app used globally. Users can take photos, record videos, add text and drawings, and send them to recipients. Messages are only available for a short time before they become inaccessible to recipients.

Netflix

Netflix is a streaming service that offers a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, anime, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. Users can watch as much as they want, whenever they want, without commercials.
